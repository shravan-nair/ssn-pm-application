Stride App

Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 29, 2026

Stride is a personal running coach app. This policy explains what data the app uses, where it goes, and what stays on your device.

What we collect

Stride does not collect, store, or transmit any personally identifiable information. Specifically:

  • Workout history — your completed workouts, dates, and durations are stored locally on your device only. They are never sent to any server.
  • Preferences — settings like coach mode are stored locally on your device only.

Third-party services

When you use the AI voice coaching feature, Stride sends coaching script text to ElevenLabs (elevenlabs.io) to generate audio. This is used solely to produce the voice coaching you hear during your run. The text sent consists of pre-written coaching phrases — no personal information is included. ElevenLabs may log these requests per their own privacy policy.

The generated audio is cached on your device so the same phrase is not re-requested on future runs.

If you disable Coach Mode, no requests are made to ElevenLabs.

Permissions

  • Audio playback — used to play voice coaching cues during your run, including when the app is in the background.

Stride does not request access to your location, camera, microphone, contacts, or any other sensitive device capability.

Data sharing

Stride does not sell, share, or transmit your data to any third party, except as described above (ElevenLabs TTS requests when coach mode is active).

Data retention

All data is stored on your device. You can delete it at any time by removing the app.

Children

Stride is not directed at children under 13 and does not knowingly collect data from them.

Changes

If this policy changes materially, the updated date at the top of this page will reflect that.
